Credit Calculator

The Credit Calculator estimates how many daily cards you need to hit your AOR and percentage goals. It takes your Past Due percentage and subtracts today’s Target Close Goal percentage, then calculates the Card Difference like this:

Cards Needed = ((Past Due % - Close Goal %) × AOR) ÷ 100.

For the Open Goal, your Now Due percentage is compared to tomorrow’s Target Open Goal percentage using the same formula. If more than 10% of your AOR is rolling, the app informs you how many cards are needed to reach that goal.

Cost Calculator

The Cost Calculator finds your total weekly cost and breaks it down into bi-weekly and monthly equivalents. First, the weekly price adds a required LDW (Liability Damage Waiver):

Weekly Price = Base Price × 1.1

Then applies sales tax like this:

Total Weekly Price = Weekly Price × 1.06 (PA Sales Tax)

If you toggle the BP (Club) fee, it adds $3.17 to the final weekly price AFTER all other calculations. The bi-weekly cost doubles the weekly total, and the monthly cost multiplies the weekly price by 4.345.
